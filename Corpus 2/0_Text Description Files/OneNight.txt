One Night is a booking app that celebrates spontaneous living by providing same night stays for a curated group of hotels in major cities. Beginning at 3pm daily, users have instantaneous access to the most remarkable hotels always at the lowest rates.  The app provides an hour by hour experiential timeline that allows users to maximize their stay experience.  One Night is based on the belief that no remarkable night should be spent in unremarkable settings. With just one swipe, your room will be waiting for you.  The Night Begins at 3pm.
