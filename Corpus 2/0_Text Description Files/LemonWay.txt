Lemon Way is a mobile wallet for your smartphone.  Lemon Way makes a secured link between your Visa or Mastercard card and your mobile phone number.  Use Lemon Way to pay your friends on their mobile phone, anywhere in Europe. A good news just in time for the European Elections ;-)  - Pay-In is Free - Person to Person payment is Free - Pay-out to your bank is free for your first 50 euros, than 2,5%
