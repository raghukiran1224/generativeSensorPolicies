This is the companion app for the Shift Card, a Visa debit card that lets you spend alternative stores of value like Coinbase bitcoin and Dwolla dollars at over 38 million merchants worldwide.  Use this app to order a Shift Card, track your spending, and receive real-time push notifications whenever your card is used.  If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us at support@shiftpayments.com.
