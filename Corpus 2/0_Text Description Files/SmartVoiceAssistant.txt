Smart Voice Assistant is pretty useful app, which let you control your phone by voice. This app ideally suited while you drive a car. Thanks to it, you won’t endanger your and other people's lives. When you receive message, this app will display and even read it if you want it. In case you want make a call, Just say key-word and your contact’s name. This app can activate loudspeaker if you want. So download this incredible app and explore more features which were implemented for you.
